Drying unit

The equipment is mainly composed of gas boilers for heating of diathermic oil, an expansion tank, circulation pumps and external heat exchangers.

The hot oil boilers are an innovative solution for the generation of heat in industrial processes where high temperatures are required.

With a low vapor pressure, moderate viscosity and high thermal stability, the fluid provides a fast and easy control of process temperatures, an essential condition in many processes to ensure uniform heating.
