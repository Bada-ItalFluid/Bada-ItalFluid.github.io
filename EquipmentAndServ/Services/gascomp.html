Gas Compression and Dehydration

Italfluid Gas Compression and Dehydration systems are employed to enhance well production or field production performances. The system is based on modular skid units, mainly composed by reciprocating compressors, from one to three compression stages.

Modularity allows to apply the system at the single well head or at manifold level to improve production of several wells.

Units whenever required can be equipped with gas engines. Fuel gas, derived from expedited process gas, makes the system self-sufficient
