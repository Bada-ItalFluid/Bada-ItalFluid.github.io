Company Overview

Our mission is to provide to our customers best in class services by means of professionalism, ethics, responsible and proactive attitude.
Italfluid aims to be a real example in upstream industry

History

Italfluid Geoenergy S.r.l. is an Italian services company that has been operating in the oil industry for over 30 years. It mainly specializes in the following fields of activity:

  • Production testing of oil wells.

  • Early production and long production test.

  • Measures and checks in wells (read out, the memory gauge).

  • Gas Desulfurization.

  • Design and mechanical engineering.

  • Design, construction and maintenance of process plants and hydrocarbon processing.

In recent years it has expanded its activities abroad in collaboration with the other companies of the group.

Company Profile

Italfluid is an integrated Service Company which integrates Fields Operating Teams with Engineering and Manufacturing divisions. Equipment like: separators, heaters, choke valves, SDV valves, sand filters, injection units, flowlines, etc., including PLC control systems, are designed and constructed inhouse. The integration Fields Teams – Engineering – Manufacturing allows the fully implementation of the continuous improvement cycle: on site experiences are transferred to the engineers, allowing them to analyse the event and optimise working procedure, process treatments and equipment design. Approved modifications are applied to the fabrications in the workshop and results, to close the loop, verified on site.
